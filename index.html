How To Play Memory Game

Memory Game is a classic and entertaining way to test and enhance your memory skills. The objective is simple: match pairs of colored squares by revealing their colors, all while remembering their positions on the grid. It's a game that challenges your cognitive abilities and offers endless fun for players of all ages.
